# OpenWhipMax

> The world's only phone app that lets you physically whip your AI into shape.

---

OpenWhipMax turns your phone into a whip controller. Swing it overhead like a cowboy, and when the app detects the crack gesture, it sends a motivating phrase to whatever's running in your terminal. It's completely unnecessary. It's absolutely the right call.

The agent Github repositroy : https://github.com/OsaidAlhomedy/OpenWhipMax-Agent
---

## How it works

1. Pair your phone with the agent running on your computer (scan a QR code)
2. Arm the app
3. Swing your phone like you mean it
4. Ctrl+C gets sent to your terminal
5. Feel powerful

That's it. There's no step 6.

---

## Safety

The app detects free-fall and automatically disarms itself if you drop your phone mid-swing. We've thought about this more than you'd expect.

Please use the wrist strap. The app will remind you exactly once, and then never mention it again because it respects your autonomy (and your floors).
